About Us

We are a platform that wants to return to bring back rock/punk/metal. The modern generations are mostly used to pop, jazz and soft tempo music. With COVID and the measures that came with it, we forgot how to have fun, enjoy ourselves and bring out our love for music! We want to remind everyone of how it was before and return to those times.
